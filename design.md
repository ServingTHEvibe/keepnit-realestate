# Keepnit Real Estate — Design Direction

## Brand
- Name: Keepnit Real Estate
- Handle: @keepnit.real.estate515
- Market: Des Moines, IA + surrounding areas
- Vibe: Premium, trustworthy, modern — luxury without being cold

## Color Palette
- **Primary**: `#1a1a2e` — Deep navy (authority, trust)
- **Accent**: `#c9a96e` — Warm gold (luxury, warmth)
- **Surface**: `#f8f5f0` — Off-white cream (softness, premium)
- **Card**: `#ffffff`
- **Text primary**: `#1a1a2e`
- **Text muted**: `#6b7280`
- **Border**: `#e5e0d8`

## Typography
- **Display**: Playfair Display — elegant serif for headlines
- **Body**: DM Sans — clean, modern sans-serif
- Scale: 5xl/4xl hero, 3xl section heads, xl card titles, base body

## Motion (GSAP)
- Hero: Full-screen video background with parallax overlay text
- Scroll: ScrollTrigger fade-up on all sections
- Numbers: CountUp animation on stats
- Cards: stagger fade-in on features grid
- Smooth scroll behavior site-wide

## Layout
- Full-width hero with video background (overlay dark gradient)
- Nav: glass morphism sticky top bar
- Sections: generous padding (py-24+), max-w-7xl centered
- Property cards: asymmetric 3-column grid
- No plain card grids — use layered, offset layouts

## Reference
- findrealestate.com: bold text over full-screen luxury imagery
- Linear/Notion: whitespace and component precision
